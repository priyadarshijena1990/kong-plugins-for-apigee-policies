2. decide how to proceed for the goals

4. according to the step to create taks, subtasks and status to-do ensuring the tasks and subtasks maintain the order and sequencing
5. then take one subtask at a time, put the status to in-progress

10. keep the already existing tasks and subtasks irrespective of status present in the todo.html and todo without deleting
11. ensure todo and todo.html both are in sync
